Three new pizzas join the menu!

The Fiery Italian

Serrano Ham, pepperoni, red and green chillies, and mozarella, all layered over our freshly-made dough and rich house tomato base, finished with a sprinkle of parsely to seal the deal.

Bad Boy BBQ

Chicken, pepperoni, sundried tomatoes, parmesan shavings, and mozarella, all layered over our freshly-made dough and a rich, smokey BBQ sauce base, finished with that all important sprinkle of parsley.

Cowboy's Dream

Cajun chicken, spicy beef, eight hour BBQ pulled pork, sweetcorn, and mozarella, all layered over our freshly-made dough and our signature BBQ base, topped off with paprika, a sprinkle of parsely, and a generous drizzle of our rich ranch dressing.
